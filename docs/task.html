Task

2024-2025

Objective

Predict point spread together with a 95% confidence interval in the 2025 NCAA conference games for Duke, NC State and UNC, Chapel Hill.

Instructions

  1. Download a copy of the google sheet here.

  2. Only edit the light blue cells to input your predictions and team name. Make a prediction for each of the 55 games.

Important

Enter all values to two decimal places.

  • Enter the point spread (home team minus away team) in the point_spread column.
  • Enter the lower bound of the 95% confidence interval in the ci_95_lb column.
  • Enter the upper bound of the 95% confidence interval in the ci_95_ub column.
  1. Click File, Download, Comma Separated Values (.csv) to download your prediction spreadsheet. Rename your file to the name of your team. Upload your file to the dropbox link here. Make sure your file type is a csv.

Evaluation

Your predictions (point estimates and confidence intervals) will be evaluated separately by two metrics described below.

  1. Mean Absolute Error

\[ \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^n \left| \mu_i - \hat{\mu}_i \right| \]

where \(\mu_i\) is the actual point spread outcome (home - away points) and \(\hat{\mu}_i\) is your predicted point spread for the \(i\)th game. The average is ta ken over all \(n = 55\) total games that you are predicting the outcome for. In words, the mean absolute error is the average number of points your predictions are away from the truth.
